Dataset (2026)

A large-scale dataset of real-world chatbot conversations in the wild, enabling research on user-chatbot interactions and safety.

PlagBench

Dataset (NAACL 2025)

A benchmark for evaluating the plagiarism behaviors of Large Language Models, assessing their integrity and originality.
Paper

Dataset (ICWSM 2025)

Benchmarking offensive text classifiers with real-life human-written (and not machine-generated) perturbations.
